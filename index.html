Smart Food Guide

Search for foods to see what to enjoy and what to limit.

🟢 Foods to Eat

  • SpinachPacked with iron, vitamins, and antioxidants.
  • BlueberriesHigh in fiber and great for brain health.
  • SalmonExcellent source of Omega-3 fatty acids.
  • AlmondsHealthy fats that keep you full longer.
  • OatsGreat for digestion and heart health.

🔴 Foods to Avoid

  • Soda & Sugary DrinksHigh in empty calories and spikes blood sugar.
  • Potato ChipsDeep-fried, high in sodium and unhealthy fats.
  • White BreadHighly processed, lacks fiber and nutrients.
  • Frozen PizzasOften loaded with preservatives and sodium.
  • MargarineCan contain trans fats which harm heart health.
